# **App Name**: Radiance Narrative

## Core Features:

- The Scholar's Interactive Archive: A comprehensive vertical timeline showcasing academic excellence from PEC (2016) to HSC (2024), including specific board positions like 2nd in board with 1256 marks, talent pull scholarships, and current status at the University of Dhaka Bengali Department.
- Cognitive Matrix & Strategy Hub: An interactive module detailing mental abilities through visualizations of strategic chess dynamics, logical deduction matrices, Sudoku variants, and linguistic mechanics used in cryptic word puzzles.
- Creative & Rhetorical Expression Stage: A dedicated multimedia player and text viewer for showcasing oratory performances, public speaking clips, nuanced poetry, and prose fiction focusing on psychological subtext.
- High-Fidelity Modeling & Arts Gallery: A premium image and video gallery designed for future modeling portfolios and current performing arts choreography, featuring high-fidelity filtering and smooth light-box transitions.
- Intellectual Inquiry Lab: Categorized sections that explain abstract conceptual models, psychological pattern research on behavioral heuristics, and philosophical thought experiments on personal identity.
- Engagement & Inquiries Vault: A background system to securely store modeling agency inquiries and visitor messages within a persistent database for future correspondence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Background color: #FDF5E6 (Old Lace) provides a warm, archival paper feel that suggests a classic literary and academic environment.
- Primary color: #000000 (Pure Black) for headers and core structural elements to ensure high-contrast editorial clarity and professional sophistication.
- Secondary/Accent color: #DC143C (Crimson) for call-to-actions, gallery highlights, and modeling portfolio buttons to evoke passion and performance.
- Tertiary color: #808000 (Olive) for scholarly subsections and research tags, reflecting wisdom and the depth of intellectual inquiry.
- Headlines: 'Playfair Display' (Serif) for a luxurious and literary feel; Body: 'PT Sans' (Sans-serif) for high legibility in dense academic or psychological descriptions.
- Luxury agency-style editorial grid with wide margins and generous white space to allow for a clean separation between academic data and artistic portfolios.
- Minimalist fine-line icons representing different spheres: a telescope for Astronomy, a Greek pillar for Philosophy, and a chess rook for Logic.
- Subtle, fluid page transitions and hover effects that simulate the elegance of flipping through a high-end fashion or literary journal.
